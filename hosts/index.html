Distinguish your rental with fine art from local artists.

Offer your guests a unique experience.

Art Box is a currated collection of fine art that your guests can browse and admire. When they find a piece they love, they scan a QR code to buy it, and take it home with them as a memento of their trip and a reminder of your property.

Support local art.

Art Box licenses artwork from local painters and photographers. Each giclée print is presented with archival-materials and comes pre-matted for easy framing.

10% of sales support local art programs in Mariposa, Oakhurst, and Groveland.

How does it work?

Simply order a box and Art Box does the rest. We’ll mail the box to your property, replenish it when inventory gets low, ensure artists get paid for their work, cover any theft, and handle returns.
