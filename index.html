Art Box

A shoppable box of local artwork

— How it works —

1

Browse a collection of hand-curated artwork

Every Art Box is a curated collection of artwork made by local artists. Every piece represents the artist’s community, local culture, and geography.

2

Purchase any piece by scanning the QR code on the back

When you find a piece that speaks to you, scan the QR code on the back with your camera. Checkout is fast and secure.

3

Take it home

That’s it! The piece you purchased is now yours and Art Box ensures the artist gets paid.
